Prepare Yourself

Before starting any creative project, it's essential to center yourself. Take a moment to breathe deeply and clear your mind. This will help you focus and set a positive intention for your creative journey.

Cut on Dotted Lines

Precision Matters

Using scissors, carefully cut along the dotted lines of your materials. This step is crucial for ensuring that your pieces fit together perfectly later on. Take your time and make sure your cuts are clean and precise.

Fold with Arrow

Shape Your Creation

Follow the indicated arrows to fold your project accurately. Folding is an essential technique that adds dimension and structure to your creation. Ensure that your folds are crisp and aligned for the best results.

Glue on Zigzags

Stick with Style

Apply glue in a zigzag pattern on the edges of your cut pieces. This not only adds a creative flair but also ensures that the pieces adhere well. Make sure to use enough glue to secure the pieces without making a mess.
